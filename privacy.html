Cookies and Tracking Technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They contain information about your browsing activity and preferences. Cookies help us recognize you, remember your preferences, and understand how you use our website.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility.

Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website, including which pages are visited most often and whether errors occur. This information helps us improve website performance.

Functional Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences and choices to provide a more personalized experience, such as language preferences or login information.

Marketing Cookies

These cookies track your browsing activity across websites to deliver targeted advertisements and measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns.

Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services such as Google Analytics, Google Ads, Facebook Pixel, and other analytics and advertising platforms. These services may set their own cookies on your device to track your behavior across multiple websites. We do not control these third-party cookies, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through your browser settings. You can choose to accept or reject cookies, or receive notification when a cookie is being sent. To manage your cookie preferences, consult your browser's help menu or visit www.allaboutcookies.org.

Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website and limit your ability to use certain features. You may also opt out of targeted advertising through the Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA) or Network Advertising Initiative (NAI).
